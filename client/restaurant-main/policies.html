Hotel Policies

Refund Policy

  • Booking deposit is refundable if cancellation is made at least 24 hours before reservation time.
  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.
  • No-show bookings may not be eligible for full refund.

Customer Conduct and Manners

  • Respect staff and other guests at all times.
  • Maintain polite behavior and avoid loud disturbances.
  • Any damage to property will be chargeable.
  • Follow hygiene and dress standards where applicable.

Terms and Conditions

  • Reservation confirmation is subject to table availability.
  • Guests must provide valid name, phone, and email while booking.
  • Customers get a waiting period of 30 minutes from reservation time.
  • If guest is late beyond 30 minutes, booking may be cancelled and allotted to another customer.
  • If service continuation is requested after late arrival, penalty charges may apply.
  • By booking, customer agrees to refund and conduct policies.
